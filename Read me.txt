Welcome to the Business Card Application!
 This application allows you to create, manage, and share digital business cards.
 With our user-friendly interface, you can input and store your business card information, create an account, and perform various card-related actions. This README document will guide you through the features and setup process of the application.

Features
User Accounts: Create a personal account to manage your business card information securely.
Business Card Information: Input and store your business card details, such as name, job title, contact information, company name, and logo.
Create, Edit, Like, and Delete Cards: Easily create new cards, modify existing ones, like cards of interest, and delete unwanted cards.
Card Pages: Access dedicated pages to view cards you've liked, cards you've created, and manage your profile.
Accessibility: Access your digital business cards from any device with an internet connection.

Getting Started
To use the Business Card Application, follow the steps below:

Prerequisites
A computer or mobile device with an internet connection.
A web browser that supports HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.
Installation
No installation is required for the Business Card Application. Simply access the application through your preferred web browser by visiting our website www.businesscardapp.com.

Account Creation
Visit the Business Card Application website.
Click on the "Sign Up" or "Create Account" button.
Fill in the required information, including your name, email address, and a secure password.
Review and accept the terms of service and privacy policy.
Click on the "Create Account" button to create your account.
Logging In
Visit the Business Card Application website.
Click on the "Log In" button.
Enter your registered email address and password.
Click on the "Log In" button to access your account.
Creating a Business Card
After logging in, you will be directed to the dashboard.
Click on the "Create New Business Card" or similar button.
Enter your business card information, including your name, job title, contact details, and company information.
Optionally, upload a logo or image for your business card.
Click on the "Save" button to store your business card.
Editing a Business Card
Access your business card from the dashboard or the "My Cards" page.
Click on the "Edit" or similar button.
Modify the desired fields of your business card, such as name, job title, or contact details.
Optionally, update the logo or image for your business card.
Click on the "Save" button to update your business card.
Liking a Business Card
Browse through the available business cards on the application.
Click on the "Like" button associated with the card of interest.
The liked card will be added to your "Liked Cards" page for future reference.
Deleting a Business Card
Access your business card from the dashboard or the "My Cards" page.
Click on the "Delete" or similar button.
Confirm the deletion when prompted.
The card will be permanently removed from your account.
Viewing Liked Cards
Access the "Liked Cards" page from the navigation menu or dashboard.
Browse through the cards you've liked.
Perform actions such as contacting the card owner or sharing the card as needed.
Viewing Created Cards
Access the "My Cards" or "Created Cards" page from the navigation menu or dashboard.
Browse through the cards you've created.
Perform actions such as editing or deleting the cards as needed.
Managing Profile
Access the "Profile" or "Account Settings" page from the navigation menu or dashboard.
Update your profile information, including name, contact details, or password.
Save the changes to update your profile.
Troubleshooting
If you encounter any issues or have questions regarding the Business Card Application, please refer to our FAQ section on the website or contact our support team at support@businesscardapp.com for further assistance.
